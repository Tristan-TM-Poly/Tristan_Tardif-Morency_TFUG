# ACCOUNT_ROLE_MEMBERSHIP_PACKET — Applied Snapshot (2026-04-14)

## Purpose
Define the first bounded packet family for accounts, roles, and memberships across public and member surfaces.

## Roles
- guest
- registered
- member
- research_pro
- institutional_partner
- admin_review

## Membership outputs
- access tier
- visible modules
- gated modules
- donation eligibility
- subscription eligibility
- review and audit scope

## Law
Account-role-membership coupling is valid only if membership growth strengthens mission continuity and public trust without weakening governance boundaries.
