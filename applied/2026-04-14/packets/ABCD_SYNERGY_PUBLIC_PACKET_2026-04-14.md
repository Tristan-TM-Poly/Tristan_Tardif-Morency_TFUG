# ABCD_SYNERGY_PUBLIC_PACKET — Applied Snapshot (2026-04-14)

## Purpose
Define how the A/B/C/D synergy families should appear on public and semi-public surfaces.

## Required packet members
- HumanPacket: explain what each synergy family means
- MachinePacket: classify family, routes, visible targets, and integration status
- AIPacket: suggest the next bounded expansion or explanation route
- ReviewPacket: block inflated exposure of immature future families
- PropagationPacket: route users toward demos, notes, collaborations, and deeper theory surfaces

## Law
The public packet is valid only if it increases legibility and conversion while preserving maturity distinctions between current and future synergy families.
