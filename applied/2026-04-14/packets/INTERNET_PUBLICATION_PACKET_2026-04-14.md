# INTERNET_PUBLICATION_PACKET — Applied Snapshot (2026-04-14)

## Purpose
Define the first bounded packet family for publication on internet surfaces.

## Publication surfaces
- public notes
- technical digests
- artifact summaries
- bounded demo explainers
- AT-1 website notes and links

## Required packet members
- HumanPacket: readable explanation
- MachinePacket: artifact refs and source ids
- ReviewPacket: maturity and claim gate
- PropagationPacket: where the publication should land next
- optional ContradictionPacket when claim risk is non-trivial

## Law
Internet publication is valid only if it publishes bounded, reviewable, source-linked outputs rather than high-amplitude unsupported claims.
