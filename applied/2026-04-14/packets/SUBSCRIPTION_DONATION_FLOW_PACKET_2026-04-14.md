# SUBSCRIPTION_DONATION_FLOW_PACKET — Applied Snapshot (2026-04-14)

## Purpose
Define the first bounded flow packet for subscriptions and donations.

## Flow stages
- discover plan or donation path
- inspect mission and access impact
- choose plan or donation amount
- review role and access consequences
- confirm and route to dashboard

## Required safeguards
- public transparency of purpose
- bounded maturity distinctions
- audit trail
- rollback/review path for account issues

## Law
Subscription and donation flows are valid only if they support the mission and platform continuity without degrading trust or coercing users.
