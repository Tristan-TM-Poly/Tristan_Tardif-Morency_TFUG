# ABCD_SYNERGY_INTEGRATION_POLICY — Applied Snapshot (2026-04-14)

## Purpose
Define how A^i synergies, B^j meta-synergies, C^k generative-meta-synergies, and D^l future generative-meta-synergies should be integrated across the TFUGA sovereign stack.

## Integration targets
- public website
- interactive demos and educational game layers
- AI-BOT NPC guidance and orchestration layers
- Yggdrasil / hypergraph surfaces
- AI-7 and TRISTAN2 runtime and packet layers
- research, thesis, publication, and propagation layers

## Core rule
A/B/C/D synergies should not remain ranking-only objects. Each strong synergy should tend toward one visible surface, one packet family, one score or review interpretation, and one propagation route.

## Priority order
1. A^i synergies into pilots and visible demos
2. B^j meta-synergies into governance, packetization, and propagation
3. C^k generative-meta-synergies into creation pipelines and public-interactive layers
4. D^l future generative-meta-synergies into staged roadmaps and bounded research programs

## Law
Integration is valid only when it increases closure and cross-layer transportability more than it increases symbolic bulk.
