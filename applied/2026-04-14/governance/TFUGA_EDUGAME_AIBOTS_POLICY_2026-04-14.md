# TFUGA_EDUGAME_AIBOTS_POLICY — Applied Snapshot (2026-04-14)

## Purpose
Define how educational game layers, AI-BOT guides, and public-interactive theory development should behave inside the TFUGA x AI-7 x TRISTAN2 stack.

## Core rule
Educational game modules must teach, test, and deepen governed theory and system understanding while remaining bounded, interpretable, and coupled to real artifact families.

## NPC doctrine
AI-BOT NPCs should:
- guide players through bounded concept paths
- suggest next admissible actions
- compare human choices with governed baselines
- surface packets, notes, and artifacts at the right time
- never bypass review, contradiction, or gate layers

## Game-law coupling
Every game module should expose:
1. one TFUGA mechanic or structural law
2. one AI-7 adaptive loop
3. one TRISTAN2 score/review/gate corridor
4. one learning or collaboration outcome

## Law
Educational game layers are valid only when they improve understanding, retention, experimentation, and theory/system development without degenerating into uncoupled spectacle.
