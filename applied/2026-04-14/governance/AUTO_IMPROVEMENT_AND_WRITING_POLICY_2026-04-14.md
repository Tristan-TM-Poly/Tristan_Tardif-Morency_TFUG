# AUTO_IMPROVEMENT_AND_WRITING_POLICY — Applied Snapshot (2026-04-14)

## Purpose
Define how automatic improvement and automatic writing should behave inside the governed TFUGA automation layer.

## Policy
- automatic improvement must be driven by automation evidence first, not symbolic preference
- automatic writing must remain subordinate to governed artifacts, score rows, review rows, and benchmark layers
- generated writing should compress state without mutating frontier hierarchy
- generated LaTeX stubs are staging outputs, not final truth artifacts
- improvement and writing automation should run from one auditable chain when possible

## Primary entrypoint
- `applied/2026-04-14/runtime/run_improvement_and_writing_bundle_2026-04-14.py`

## Expected outputs
- automation digest
- generated artifact catalog
- auto improvement queue
- auto writing package
- LaTeX-ready writing stub
- improvement and writing bundle reports

## Law
Automatic improvement and automatic writing are successful only when they increase governability, clarity, and repairability at the same time.
