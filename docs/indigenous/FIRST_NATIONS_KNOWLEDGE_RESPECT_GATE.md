# First Nations / Indigenous Knowledge RespectGate

Status: S3-ethical-documentation-architecture.

This document defines a conservative RespectGate for any TFUGA / AI-7 / HGFM work involving First Nations, Inuit, Metis, or other Indigenous knowledge systems. It is not a permission to ingest knowledge. It is a blocking gate that prevents extraction, overclaiming, unauthorized translation, or commercial use without agreement.

## Core law

Indigenous knowledge is not raw data. It is living, situated, relational, governed, and protocol-bound.

## Gate equation

UseAllowed(X) = PublicOrPermitted(X) AND ContextPreserved(X) AND CommunityBenefit(X) AND Attribution(X) AND PurposeLimited(X) AND WithdrawalPossible(X).

If any term is false, use is blocked or paused.

## RespectGate colors

GREEN: public, non-sensitive, attributed, educational, low-risk.

YELLOW: ambiguous, community-specific, contextual, needs review or permission.

RED: restricted, confidential, non-consented, sensitive, site-specific, or monetized without agreement.

## Status ladder

K0: vague idea.
K1: public source identified.
K2: general educational summary.
K3: safe public teaching artifact with attribution and limitations.
K4: community-reviewed or language-holder-reviewed artifact.
K5: formal partnership, benefit-sharing, community governance.
K6: stable co-governed protocol with withdrawal and audit rights.

## Automatic blockers

Block any proposed use involving restricted teachings, confidential material, community-specific content without permission, sensitive site information, unauthorized translation, commercial use without agreement, or claims that TFUGA owns, absorbs, restores, or canonizes Indigenous knowledge.

## Allowed low-risk uses

Allowed only with attribution and context: public principles such as reciprocity, relationality, long-term responsibility, data sovereignty, and community engagement; public educational summaries; citations to public guidance such as OCAP/PCAP, CARE, TCPS 2 Chapter 9, and UNDRIP; design-pattern analogies that do not claim equivalence or ownership.

## TFUGA/HGFM integration

FNK-HGFM = RespectGate o ConsentGate o OCAP/CARE o TranslationGate o CommunityValidation o DCT++ o EvidenceGate.

## Hard law

Do not extract. Do not absorb. Do not monetize. Build protocols that can receive knowledge only when communities choose to share it.
