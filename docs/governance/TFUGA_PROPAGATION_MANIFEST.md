## Status ceiling

Documentation alone <= S3/T3/K3.
Real data or receipt evidence required for S4/T4.
Community or appropriate review required for K4.
Independent reproduction or repeated customers required for S5/T5.
Stable minimal fertile reuse required for S6/T6.

## Blocked propagation

Do not propagate:

- guaranteed passive income;
- autonomous revenue;
- Stripe/payment mutation without receipt;
- physical/material validation without data;
- cultural ownership or absorption claims;
- sensitive community knowledge;
- claims that AI replaces human/community review;
- auto-merge or auto-deploy rules.

## Reusable propagation packet

Every propagated module should include:

- README;
- status line;
- DCT++ packet;
- claims register;
- evidence schema or evidence checklist;
- blocked-claims section;
- CI governance check;
- next least-action route;
- human review path.

## Hard law

Propagate gates, not hype. Propagate structure, not ownership. Propagate evidence paths, not claims.
