# AI-7 Acceleration Engine

Status: S3/T3 governed acceleration architecture.

Purpose: accelerate the number and quality of AI agents, theories, systems, applications, and reusable artifacts without accelerating unsupported claims, hidden actions, unsafe autonomy, or uncontrolled complexity.

## Core law

Acceleration is allowed only when proof, logs, rollback, reuse, and gates accelerate at least as fast as generation.

## Acceleration equation

AI7_Acceleration = HumanReviewGate o UnifiedGovernanceCI o EvidenceGate o DCT++ o {AgentFactory + ClaimRouter + TheoryPacketizer + SystemBridgeFactory + ApplicationMatrix + GovernanceDashboard}.

## Acceleration outputs

The engine may produce:

- AgentSpec: bounded AI role with allowed/blocked actions and tests.
- TheoryPacket: intuition, equation, assumptions, invariants, tests, falsifiers, applications.
- SystemBridgePacket: theory-to-system bridge with objects, invariants, failure modes, prototype path.
- ApplicationPacket: domain, user, impact, risk, evidence, next action.
- ClaimRoute: claim type, required gate, max status without evidence, missing evidence.
- GovernanceDashboardCard: status, blockers, next least-action route.

## Reproduction throttle

An agent or theory may spawn a derivative only if:

ValueGain > GovernanceCost + DuplicationCost + RiskCost.

Where ValueGain includes proof quality, reuse, clarity, test coverage, and impact.

## Blocked acceleration

Do not accelerate:

- auto-merge;
- spending or payment control;
- unsupported scientific or revenue claims;
- cultural/community knowledge extraction;
- hidden data access;
- public launch without review;
- agent self-replication without spec, test, ledger, and rollback.

## Status ceiling

- Acceleration architecture and dry-run packets: S3/T3 maximum.
- Revenue promotion requires receipt or sponsor log.
- Scientific promotion requires data, controls, uncertainty, and reproduction path.
- Cultural/community promotion requires review, permission, and benefit-back.

## Hard law

Accelerate agents, theories, systems, and applications only by accelerating their evidence passports.
