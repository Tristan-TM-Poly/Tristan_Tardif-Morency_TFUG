# Private Beta Outreach

Status: T3 outreach draft for human review.

Purpose: help Tristan become known through careful reviewer conversations, not spam or inflated claims.

## Target reviewers

- engineering physics students;
- spectroscopy / Raman learners;
- research-methods teachers;
- open-source scientific tooling builders;
- lab assistants or teaching assistants;
- people interested in evidence-gated AI workflows.

## Outreach message

Hi, I am testing a small local-first scientific reporting kit: OmniSpectral EvidenceGate Starter Kit.

It helps turn Raman-like CSV data into structured peak/AUC/uncertainty reports plus a DCT++ claim ledger.

It does not claim to prove new physics, validate materials, or guarantee discoveries. It is a reproducible reporting and evidence-structuring tool.

Would you be willing to review the page or test the starter pack privately and give honest feedback?

## Follow-up message

Thank you for looking at it. The main thing I want to know is whether the product promise is clear, whether the deliverables feel useful, and what would make it more credible or easier to use.

## Feedback questions

1. Do you understand what this kit does in under 30 seconds?
2. What part feels most useful?
3. What part feels confusing or too ambitious?
4. Would you use it for learning, teaching, or research workflow support?
5. What proof, demo, or screenshot would make it more credible?
6. Would you pay, sponsor, or recommend it if polished?

## Evidence rule

Interest is T3 evidence. Payment or sponsor receipt is T4 candidate. Do not claim revenue without receipt.

## Anti-spam rule

No mass DM. Start with 5-10 relevant people and personalize each message.
