## Post 1: EvidenceGate

I am building TFUGA / AI-7 / HGFM, a governed research ecosystem for turning ambitious scientific ideas into auditable artifacts.

The central rule is simple: no claim gets promoted without the right evidence gate.

Science claims need data and uncertainty.
Revenue claims need real receipts.
Cultural/community knowledge claims need permission, attribution, and benefit-back.
Software claims need tests and reproducibility.

Current work: ClaimRouter, EvidenceGate, DCT++, continuous governance, and OmniSpectral-style reporting tools.

Evidence before hype.

## Post 3: ClaimRouter

A recurring problem in AI-assisted research is that every claim gets treated like the same kind of claim.

They are not the same.

A software claim needs tests.
A scientific claim needs data.
A revenue claim needs receipts.
A cultural/community knowledge claim needs permission and review.
A deployment claim needs audit logs and rollback.

That is why I am building a ClaimRouter for TFUGA / AI-7 / HGFM: every claim goes to the correct gate before promotion.
