## The five zones
### 1. Stabilized knowledge
Formulated, tested, transmissible, reusable knowledge with bounded validity.

### 2. Partial knowledge
Partially modeled, partially tested, not yet closed knowledge.

### 3. Open unknowns
Known unknowns. Clearly recognized open questions.

### 4. Lost knowledge
Forgotten, fragmented, recoded, or partially surviving knowledge.

### 5. Invisible unknowns
Unknown unknowns. Questions not yet properly formulated.
