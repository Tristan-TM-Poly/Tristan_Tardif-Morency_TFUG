## Promotion rule

- Interest without payment: T3 evidence.
- One paid receipt or sponsor log: T4 candidate.
- Three independent customers: T5 candidate.
- 30-90 day repeatable low-maintenance sales: T6 candidate.

## Blocked behavior

Do not claim passive income, income guarantees, investment returns, or autonomous revenue. Do not publish customer private data.
