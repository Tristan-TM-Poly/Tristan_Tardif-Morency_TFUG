## Hero
OmniSpectral EvidenceGate Starter Kit

Turn spectra into structured evidence reports, without pretending your data proves more than it does.

## What it does
Load a Raman-like CSV locally, extract peaks and AUC-style features, structure uncertainty and claims, and generate a DCT++ report plus claim ledger.

## What it does not do
It does not prove TFUGA, validate superconductivity, replace laboratory calibration, or guarantee material discoveries.
