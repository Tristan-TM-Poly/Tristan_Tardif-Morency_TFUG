# Support, Refund, and Privacy Notes

Status: draft policy notes for human review before public selling.

## Support scope

The OmniSpectral EvidenceGate Starter Kit is delivered as a local-first digital research/education kit. Support should cover:

- help opening the files;
- clarification of installation instructions;
- interpretation of the included demo workflow;
- bug reports for files included in the kit;
- missing-file or corrupted-download issues.

Support should not cover:

- guaranteed research outcomes;
- claims that a spectrum proves TFUGA, superconductivity, or material performance;
- custom laboratory calibration unless sold as a separate service;
- legal, tax, medical, financial, or regulated engineering advice.

## Refund note draft

Suggested public wording:

> This is a digital research/education kit. If the files are inaccessible, corrupted, or materially different from the delivery manifest, contact support within 7 days for repair, replacement, or refund review. Because this is a downloadable digital product, refunds are not guaranteed after successful delivery, but support will be provided in good faith.

## Privacy note draft

Suggested public wording:

> Do not upload confidential, medical, proprietary, or personally identifying spectra unless you have permission to share them. The starter kit is designed for local analysis. If future hosted services are offered, a separate privacy policy will be required before accepting user data.

## Human review gate

Before public checkout:

- [ ] support contact exists;
- [ ] refund wording reviewed;
- [ ] privacy wording reviewed;
- [ ] product delivery manifest matches the paid offer;
- [ ] no guaranteed-income, guaranteed-discovery, or physical-validation claim appears on the sales page.
