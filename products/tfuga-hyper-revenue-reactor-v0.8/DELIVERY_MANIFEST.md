# Delivery Manifest

Status: draft delivery contract for the OmniSpectral EvidenceGate Starter Kit.

## Product ID

`omnispectral-evidencegate-starter-kit-v0.8`

## Buyer receives

Starter tier should include:

- `README.md` with installation and usage notes;
- demo Raman-like CSV data;
- one notebook or script for local AUC/peak extraction demo;
- DCT++ report template;
- EvidenceGate checklist;
- claim ledger template;
- metadata schema/example;
- safe-claims guide.

Pro tier may add:

- CLI wrapper or batch script;
- report generation examples;
- additional templates;
- changelog and upgrade notes.

Lab/Education tier may add:

- classroom/lab usage guide;
- instructor notes;
- one support session or async support window, if explicitly offered.

## Buyer does not receive

- guaranteed research discoveries;
- guaranteed revenue;
- proof of TFUGA, superconductivity, or material performance;
- hosted data processing unless a hosted service is separately launched;
- legal, tax, medical, financial, or regulated engineering advice.

## Delivery evidence

For T4 revenue evidence, record:

- product package version;
- delivery timestamp;
- delivery method;
- anonymized customer or sponsor identifier;
- receipt/payment reference;
- refund/support status.
