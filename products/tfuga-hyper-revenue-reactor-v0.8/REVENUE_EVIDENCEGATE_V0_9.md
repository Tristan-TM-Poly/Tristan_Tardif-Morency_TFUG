# Revenue EvidenceGate v0.9

Status: draft gate layered on top of HyperRevenue Reactor v0.8.

## Gate equation

`RevenueStatus = EvidenceGate(product + checkout + delivery + receipt + support + privacy + feedback)`

## Levels

- T0: raw revenue idea
- T1: product concept
- T2: local artifact
- T3: review-ready page/PR/brief
- T4: first real receipt or sponsor log
- T5: three or more independent customers or repeatable purchases
- T6: stable semi-passive 30-90 day low-maintenance flow

## Current state

Current PR state is T3: productization plan + files + draft PR + Drive brief.

## T4 lock

T4 requires all of:

- product artifact exists;
- delivery path exists;
- pricing page or offer exists;
- refund/support/privacy notes exist;
- human review approval exists;
- payment processor link or sponsor mechanism exists;
- first customer receipt or sponsor log exists;
- support contact exists;
- privacy/terms checked.

## T5 lock

T5 requires T4 plus:

- at least three independent customers or sponsors;
- repeat purchase, retention, or second-cycle interest;
- basic accounting export;
- maintenance/update policy;
- support load measured.

## T6 lock

T6 requires T5 plus:

- 30-90 day stable low-maintenance revenue;
- support load below target;
- refund rate below target;
- no unresolved compliance or safety blocker;
- versioned delivery pipeline.

## Blocked claims

- guaranteed passive income;
- automatic revenue without customers;
- risk-free return;
- autonomous bank/payment control;
- legal/tax/privacy compliance already solved;
- public proof of revenue without receipt/log evidence.
