# Private Beta R3 -> R4 Checklist

Status: T3 private-beta checklist for human review.

Purpose: define the smallest safe route from product candidate R3 to revenue evidence candidate R4 for OmniSpectral EvidenceGate Starter Kit.

## Core law

R4 begins only when a real buyer or sponsor exists and a receipt or sponsor log is recorded. Interest, praise, or AI confidence is not R4.

## R3 readiness

- Product page or private-beta page exists.
- Deliverables are clearly named.
- No guaranteed revenue claim.
- No validated physics/material claim.
- Support contact is defined.
- Refund/cancellation note is drafted.
- Privacy/data-handling note is drafted.
- Delivery process is documented.
- Human review approves the beta wording.

## R4 evidence requirements

- real buyer or sponsor;
- receipt ID hash or sponsor log;
- date/time;
- amount or non-cash sponsor value;
- delivered artifact reference;
- support path reference;
- privacy/refund note reference;
- anonymized ledger entry;
- human confirmation.

## Blocked claims before R4

- generated passive income;
- autonomous revenue;
- market validation;
- proven buyer demand;
- stable revenue channel;
- product-market fit;
- public launch success.

## Private beta sequence

1. Review product copy.
2. Route all public claims through ClaimRouter.
3. Confirm support/privacy/refund notes.
4. Select 5-10 relevant reviewers manually.
5. Send personalized, non-spam private beta message.
6. Record feedback packets.
7. If payment/sponsorship occurs, record anonymized receipt/sponsor log.
8. Deliver artifact.
9. Record delivery evidence.
10. Promote only to R4-candidate until reviewed.

## Hard law

No receipt, no R4. No delivery, no revenue validation. No support/privacy/refund path, no public selling.
