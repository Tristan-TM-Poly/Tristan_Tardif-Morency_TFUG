# Go-To-Market Private Beta Plan

Status: `T3-private-beta-plan`.

This plan converts the OmniSpectral EvidenceGate Starter Kit from review-ready artifact into a private beta candidate. It does not claim revenue, customer validation, or public launch.

## Beta objective

Find whether real humans understand the product promise and whether at least one person is willing to pay, sponsor, or seriously test it.

## Target beta users

1. engineering/physics student needing spectral analysis templates;
2. lab assistant or TA needing report templates;
3. open-source supporter interested in reproducible science tooling;
4. researcher who wants DCT++ style claim ledgers;
5. educator who wants a local-first teaching kit.

## Outreach message draft

```text
Hi, I’m testing a small local-first research kit: OmniSpectral EvidenceGate Starter Kit.
It turns Raman-like CSV data into structured peak/AUC/uncertainty reports plus a DCT++ claim ledger.
It does not claim to prove any new physics; it is a reproducible reporting/evidence tool.
Would you be willing to review the product page or test the starter pack privately?
```

## Beta funnel

```text
private message -> product page draft -> demo or preview -> checkout/sponsor interest -> delivery -> feedback -> T4 evidence if paid
```

## Evidence records

For each beta conversation, record:

- anonymized prospect ID;
- segment;
- offer shown;
- objection;
- willingness to pay;
- requested feature;
- whether payment/sponsor occurred;
- support load estimate.

## Success criteria

- 5 meaningful responses;
- 3 people understand the product without live explanation;
- 1 person expresses willingness to pay or sponsor;
- 1 real receipt/sponsor log moves the system to T4-candidate.

## Failure criteria

- users cannot understand the product;
- users think it claims to prove TFUGA or superconductivity;
- support load is too high;
- no one sees immediate utility.

## Next least action

Make one simple checkout or sponsor mechanism only after human review of support, refund, privacy, and delivery notes.
